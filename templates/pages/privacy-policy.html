Privacy Policy

Your kitchen data should stay clear, protected, and useful.

This Privacy Policy explains what information SousChefAI collects, how we use it, and the choices you have when using our pantry, recipe, account, and subscription features.

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Privacy at a glance

  • We use your data to run SousChefAI and personalize recipe recommendations.
  • Payment details are handled by secure payment providers, not stored by us.
  • You can contact us about access, correction, deletion, or privacy questions.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, account credentials, pantry ingredients, recipe preferences, saved recipes, and messages you send through support or contact channels.

We may also collect usage information, including pages visited, feature activity, device/browser details, and approximate technical information needed to keep the website secure and reliable.

2. How we use your information

We use information to provide, maintain, and improve SousChefAI, including to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate recipe ideas based on pantry items, preferences, and selected filters.
  • Save recipes, pantry data, and account settings.
  • Process subscriptions, enforce plan limits, and provide billing support.
  • Respond to support requests, troubleshoot issues, and protect against abuse.

3. AI-generated content

SousChefAI may process ingredients, preferences, recipe prompts, and related cooking context to generate recommendations. Avoid entering sensitive personal information into recipe prompts or free-text fields unless it is necessary for your request.

4. How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website, such as hosting, analytics, email, payment processing, authentication, and AI infrastructure providers.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect the rights and safety of users, or to investigate fraud, abuse, or security issues.

5. Payments and subscriptions

Subscription payments are processed through third-party payment providers. SousChefAI does not store full card numbers. We may receive payment status, plan, billing interval, and customer identifiers needed to activate and manage subscriptions.

6. Your choices

You can update account information through your account settings where available. You may also contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy-related assistance. Some information may need to be retained for legal, billing, security, or operational reasons.

7. Security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so we encourage you to use a strong password and keep your login details private.

We retain information for as long as needed to provide SousChefAI, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain reliable service.

8. Children's privacy

SousChefAI is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date above and, when appropriate, provide additional notice in the product.

10. Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, email us at privacy@souschefai.com.
